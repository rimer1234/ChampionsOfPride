RuPaul

1960 - Present

Early Life
Born on November 17, 1960, in San Diego, California, as RuPaul Andre Charles.
Was one of four siblings in a family that frequently moved around due to his father's work as a chemical plant operator.
Began experimenting with drag in his teenage years and eventually moved to Atlanta to pursue a career as a drag performer.
Achievements
Became widely known in the 1990s as a drag performer, singer, and actor.
Host and executive producer of the reality competition show "RuPaul's Drag Race", which premiered in 2009 and has become a cultural phenomenon.
Has released numerous albums and singles, including the hit single "Supermodel (You Better Work)".
Received a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in 2018.
Has won multiple Emmy Awards for "RuPaul's Drag Race".
Challenges
Expereinced a volatile childhood in which he had to act as amediator in his family.
He contemplated suicide when he first moved to New York in 1984.
He struggled with substane abuse due to his early life.
Personal Life
Has been open about his gender identity, describing himself as a "genderfluid drag queen".
Has been in a relationship with Australian rancher Georges LeBar since 1994, and the couple married in 2017.
Is an advocate for LGBTQ+ rights and has been involved in various charities and organizations supporting the community.
